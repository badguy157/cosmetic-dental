Common questions

Does it hurt?

Most of what we do doesn't hurt. If something might feel strange, we'll tell you first. We also have ways to help you stay comfy.

How much does it cost?

It depends on what you need. We'll give you a clear price before we start. No surprises.

How long does it take?

Some things take one visit. Others take a few weeks. We'll let you know what to expect.

Will my smile look natural?

Yes! We make sure your teeth match your face. People will notice your smile, not your dental work.

What if I'm nervous?

That's okay! Many people feel that way. Our team is kind and patient. We go at your pace.
