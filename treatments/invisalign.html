Invisalign

Clear trays that straighten teeth. No metal braces needed. Most people won't even know you're wearing them.

Who Invisalign is for

  • You want to straighten your teeth without metal braces
  • You have crooked, crowded, or gapped teeth
  • You need to fix a bite issue (overbite, underbite, crossbite)
  • You want a discreet treatment that fits your lifestyle
  • You're willing to wear aligners 20-22 hours per day

The process

1

Consultation & 3D scan

We take digital scans of your teeth and create a 3D model. You'll see a preview of how your smile will look after treatment.

2

Custom aligners

Your custom clear aligners are made just for you. Each set gently moves your teeth a little closer to their final position.

3

Wear & switch

You wear each set of aligners for 1-2 weeks, then switch to the next set. You can remove them for eating, brushing, and special occasions.

4

Regular checkups

We see you every 6-8 weeks to monitor progress and give you your next sets of aligners. Most treatments take 12-18 months.

5

Retainer

After treatment, you'll wear a retainer at night to keep your teeth in their new positions. Your straight smile is complete!

What to expect

Week 1: Consultation

Digital scans and treatment planning with 3D preview.

Weeks 2-3: Production

Your custom aligners are manufactured and shipped to us.

Months 1-18: Treatment

Wear aligners daily, switch every 1-2 weeks, check in every 6-8 weeks.

After treatment: Retention

Wear retainers at night to maintain your new smile.

Common questions

Will Invisalign hurt?

You might feel some pressure when you start a new set of aligners—that means they're working! It's not painful, just a little uncomfortable for a day or two. Most people adjust quickly.

Can people tell I'm wearing them?

Nope! The aligners are clear and virtually invisible. Most people won't notice unless you tell them. It's one of the biggest benefits over traditional braces.

What if I lose an aligner?

Contact us right away. We might have you move to the next set early, or we can order a replacement. Try to keep aligners in their case when you're not wearing them to avoid losing them.

Do I have to wear them all the time?

You should wear them 20-22 hours a day for best results. Take them out to eat, drink anything except water, brush, and floss. The more you wear them, the faster your treatment goes.

How much does Invisalign cost?

Cost depends on how complex your case is and how long treatment takes. Many patients pay similar prices to traditional braces. Check our pricing guide for more details.
