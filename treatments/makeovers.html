Who smile makeovers are for

  • You have multiple cosmetic concerns you want to address
  • You want a complete transformation of your smile
  • You're ready to invest in your confidence and appearance
  • You want a personalized plan combining different treatments
  • You have a special event or milestone coming up

The process

1

Initial consultation

We talk about what you don't like about your smile and what you want to change. We listen to your goals and concerns.

2

Comprehensive exam

We take photos, X-rays, and 3D scans of your teeth. This helps us see the full picture and plan the best approach.

3

Custom plan

We create a personalized makeover plan that might include whitening, veneers, implants, Invisalign, or bonding—whatever you need to get your dream smile.

4

Phased treatment

We complete your treatments in a logical order. Some can be done together, others need to be staged. We'll guide you through every step.

5

Final reveal

Once all treatments are complete, we make any final adjustments. Then you get to see and enjoy your stunning new smile!

Common questions

How long does a smile makeover take?

It depends on what treatments you need. Some makeovers are done in a few weeks (like whitening + bonding). Others take several months if you need implants or Invisalign. We'll give you a clear timeline during planning.

Will I look natural or "too perfect"?

Your smile will look natural and suit your face. We work with you to choose the right shade, shape, and size for your teeth. The goal is a beautiful smile that still looks like you.

Can I see what my smile will look like before we start?

Yes! We use digital smile design to show you a preview of your results. You can see how different options will look and make changes before we begin any treatment.

What if I only want to fix one or two things?

That's fine! A makeover can be as big or small as you want. We'll only recommend what you actually need and what fits your budget. No pressure to do more than you're comfortable with.

How much does a smile makeover cost?

Cost varies widely based on which treatments you choose. We'll give you a detailed quote after your consultation so you know exactly what to expect. See our pricing guide for treatment ranges.
