Veneers

Thin covers for your front teeth. They fix chips, stains, and gaps. Great if you want a perfect smile fast.

Book consultation
← Back to Treatments

Who veneers are for

  • You have stained teeth that don't respond to whitening
  • You want to fix chips, cracks, or minor misalignment
  • You have gaps between your teeth you'd like to close
  • You're looking for a fast way to transform your smile
  • You want a natural-looking, long-lasting solution

The process

1

Consultation

We talk about your smile goals and check if veneers are right for you. We'll show you what your new smile could look like.

2

Preparation

We gently remove a thin layer of enamel from your teeth. Then we take precise impressions to create your custom veneers.

3

Temporary veneers

You'll wear temporary veneers while your permanent ones are being crafted in our lab. This usually takes about 2 weeks.

4

Placement

We carefully bond your new veneers to your teeth. We make sure they fit perfectly and look completely natural.

5

Follow-up

We check in after a few weeks to make sure everything feels great. We'll give you tips on how to care for your new smile.

What to expect

Week 1: Consultation

Meet with us to discuss your goals and plan your treatment.

Week 2: Prep & impressions

We prepare your teeth and create molds for your custom veneers.

Weeks 3-4: Crafting

Your veneers are custom-made in our lab while you wear temporaries.

Week 5: Final placement

We bond your permanent veneers and reveal your new smile.

Common questions

Do veneers hurt?

No. We use local anesthetic during the prep work, so you won't feel any pain. After the appointment, you might have slight sensitivity for a few days, but it goes away quickly.

How long do veneers last?

With good care, veneers typically last 10-15 years. Brush and floss normally, avoid biting hard objects, and come in for regular checkups to keep them in great shape.

Can I still eat normally with veneers?

Yes! Veneers are strong and durable. You can eat most foods without worry. Just avoid biting very hard items like ice or hard candy, which could damage them.

Will my veneers look fake?

Not at all. We custom-make each veneer to match the shape and color that looks natural for you. Most people won't know you have veneers unless you tell them.

How much do veneers cost?

The cost depends on how many teeth you're treating and the materials we use. We'll give you a clear price estimate during your consultation. View our pricing guide for general ranges.
